The Problem We Solve

The Clinical Trials System Is Broken for Early-Stage Innovators

82% of biotech capital is controlled by just 11 mega-funds. The median cost to reach FDA approval is $2 billion, yet most startups will never raise more than $30M. 86% of all capital flows to late-stage trials, leaving Preclinical and Phase I programs starved. The result: more than 90% of startups never raise enough to complete a clinical trial, regardless of scientific merit. The system rewards proximity to capital, not quality of science.
